YOU ONLY HAVE TO DO THESE 3 THINGS

1) PUBLISH THE WEBSITE
Recommended easiest option: Netlify Drop.
- Unzip this folder.
- Open Netlify Drop in your browser.
- Drag the entire “apex_forge_complete” folder into the upload box.
- Netlify gives you a public website link.
- Test the website on your phone.

Important: Upload the whole folder, not only index.html.

2) ACTIVATE THE ASSESSMENT EMAIL — ONE TIME ONLY
- Open the live website.
- Select any plan.
- Click “I’ve Paid — Complete Assessment”.
- Fill a test assessment using your own email and submit it.
- FormSubmit sends an activation email to h.harshul08@gmail.com.
- Open Gmail and click the activation/confirmation link.
- After this one-time activation, every new client assessment will automatically arrive in your Gmail.
- The client will automatically receive the welcome email.

Check Spam/Promotions if the activation email is not visible.

3) TEST THE COMPLETE CLIENT JOURNEY
- Select the 3-month plan.
- Confirm ₹6,000 appears.
- Scan the QR only if doing a real test payment.
- Click “I’ve Paid”.
- Complete the assessment.
- Confirm:
  a) The assessment reaches h.harshul08@gmail.com.
  b) The client email receives the welcome message.
  c) The thank-you page opens.
  d) The WhatsApp confirmation works.

IMPORTANT LIMITATION
The QR is a general UPI QR. The website cannot independently know whether payment was successful.
The client must enter the UPI transaction ID, and you must verify it in Razorpay/your bank before starting coaching.

For fully automatic payment verification later:
- Create separate Razorpay Payment Links for ₹6,000, ₹10,000 and ₹17,000.
- Then use Razorpay webhooks or a no-code automation platform.
- This requires a payment-specific setup and is not safely possible using only a QR image.
