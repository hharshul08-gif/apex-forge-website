Behind Built Beyond

I understand the pressure because I have lived it.

I am Harshul Mehta—a Chartered Accountant, former Big 4 professional, certified nutrition coach and fitness specialist with over a decade of experience.

During my time in Big 4 firms, I experienced long workdays, aggressive client deadlines, and constant business travel firsthand. I know how easy it is to let health slip while trying to stay healthy and build a career.

Built Beyond was created because traditional fitness advice is designed for people with unlimited time—not professionals with demanding careers. My approach builds health systems that work during your busiest weeks, not just your perfect ones.
