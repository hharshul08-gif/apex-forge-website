Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before joining.

Built Beyond is designed to make online coaching simple, clear and accessible from anywhere.

Is the coaching completely online?

Yes. Assessment, nutrition guidance, workout planning, progress reviews and support are delivered digitally through WhatsApp, email, video calls or shared documents.

Can I join from outside India?

Yes. Built Beyond is available worldwide. Your plan can be adapted to your local foods, schedule, time zone and available training facilities.

Who is the coaching for?

It is open to professionals and employees, business owners and self-employed individuals, adults 40+, and complete beginners. Plans are adjusted to your current level rather than expecting you to already be fit.

Do I need access to a gym?

No. Your plan can be designed for a gym, home workouts or a mix of both, depending on your equipment, preferences and routine.

Are supplements compulsory?

No. Whole foods and sustainable habits come first. Supplements are optional and considered only when appropriate for your diet, goals and health circumstances.

How soon will I receive my plan?

After payment and completion of the detailed assessment, onboarding normally begins within two business days. Timelines may vary when additional health information is required.

Will the plan consider my food preferences?

Yes. Vegetarian, eggitarian and non-vegetarian preferences can be accommodated, along with your routine, cuisine, meal frequency and practical constraints.

Is this suitable if I have a medical condition?

You should disclose all medical conditions and medications during assessment. Coaching is not a substitute for medical treatment, and medical clearance may be requested where appropriate.
