EASIEST METHOD
Tell ChatGPT the new discount and ask for an updated website ZIP.

TO EDIT IT YOURSELF
1. Open index.html in TextEdit or VS Code.
2. Press Command + F and search for “20% OFF”.
3. Change the original price, offer price and offer label as required.
4. Search for each button’s data-price value and update it to the amount the client must pay.
5. Save the file.
6. Drag the complete apex_forge_v3 folder into Netlify Production Deploys.

TO REMOVE THE DISCOUNT
For every plan:
- Delete the old-price line.
- Delete the discount-badge line.
- Restore the normal price in the price line.
- Restore the normal amount in data-price.
- Remove the sale-banner section near the top of index.html.
